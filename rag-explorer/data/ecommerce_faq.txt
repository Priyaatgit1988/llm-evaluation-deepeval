ShopSmart E-Commerce FAQ and Knowledge Base

SHIPPING POLICY
We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days and is free on orders over $50. Express shipping costs $9.99 and delivers within 1-2 business days. International shipping starts at $14.99 and takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination country. All orders are shipped with tracking numbers that are emailed to you once your package is dispatched. We ship to over 50 countries worldwide.

RETURN AND REFUND POLICY
Our return policy allows customers to return items within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging with all tags attached. To initiate a return, log into your account and go to the Orders section, then select the item you wish to return. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error. For defective items, we offer free return shipping and a full refund or exchange.

PAYMENT METHODS
We accept all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. We also accept PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. For orders over $100, we offer installment payments through Affirm with 0% APR for qualifying customers. All transactions are protected with 256-bit SSL encryption. Gift cards can be purchased in denominations of $25, $50, $100, and $200.

PRODUCT CATEGORIES
Electronics: We carry a wide range of electronics including wireless headphones, laptop stands, phone chargers, smart home devices, and portable speakers. All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty.
Footwear: Our footwear collection includes running shoes, casual sneakers, hiking boots, and sandals. We offer sizes from US 5 to US 15 and provide a size guide on each product page.
Fitness: Stay active with our fitness gear including yoga mats, resistance bands, water bottles, dumbbells, and fitness trackers. All fitness products come with a 90-day satisfaction guarantee.
Grocery: Our organic grocery section features coffee beans, teas, snacks, and superfoods sourced from sustainable farms worldwide.
Accessories: Browse our accessories including backpacks, sunglasses, watches, wallets, and phone cases.

CUSTOMER LOYALTY PROGRAM
Join our ShopSmart Rewards program to earn points on every purchase. Earn 1 point per dollar spent. 100 points equals a $5 reward. Members get early access to sales, exclusive discounts, and free shipping on all orders. Birthday month bonus: earn double points during your birthday month. VIP members (500+ points) get access to exclusive products and priority customer support.

ORDER TRACKING
Track your order by logging into your account and visiting the Orders page. You can also track using the tracking number sent to your email. Our tracking system updates in real-time and shows: order confirmed, processing, shipped, out for delivery, and delivered statuses. If your package shows as delivered but you haven't received it, please contact our support team within 48 hours.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT
Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 9 PM EST, and Saturday-Sunday, 10 AM to 6 PM EST. You can reach us via live chat on our website, email at support@shopsmart.com, or phone at 1-800-SHOP-SMART. Average response time for email is 4 hours. Live chat typically connects within 2 minutes. For urgent issues, phone support is recommended.

SIZE GUIDE
For clothing items, we follow standard US sizing. We recommend measuring your chest, waist, and hips and comparing to our size chart available on each product page. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit. Our customer reviews often include sizing feedback to help you choose.

SUSTAINABILITY COMMITMENT
ShopSmart is committed to sustainability. We use recycled packaging materials for all shipments. Our carbon offset program neutralizes the environmental impact of shipping. We partner with eco-friendly brands and clearly label sustainable products. By 2025, we aim to have 50% of our product catalog from sustainable sources.
