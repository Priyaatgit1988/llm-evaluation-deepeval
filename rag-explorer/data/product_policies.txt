ShopSmart Product Policies and Warranty Information

WARRANTY COVERAGE
All electronic products sold on ShopSmart come with a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty. This covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. Extended warranty plans are available for purchase at checkout, offering coverage for up to 3 years. Warranty claims can be filed through your account dashboard or by contacting customer support.

PRICE MATCH GUARANTEE
We offer a price match guarantee within 14 days of purchase. If you find the same product at a lower price from an authorized retailer, we will match that price and refund the difference. The product must be identical in brand, model, and condition. Price match does not apply to clearance items, marketplace sellers, or auction sites.

PRODUCT AUTHENTICITY
All products sold on ShopSmart are 100% authentic and sourced directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors. We do not sell refurbished items unless clearly labeled. Each product listing includes detailed specifications, high-resolution images, and verified customer reviews.

BULK ORDERING
For bulk orders of 10 or more units, we offer volume discounts ranging from 5% to 20% depending on the quantity and product category. Contact our business sales team at business@shopsmart.com for custom quotes. Bulk orders typically ship within 5-7 business days.

GIFT SERVICES
We offer gift wrapping for $4.99 per item. Gift messages can be included at no extra cost. Gift receipts are available and do not show the purchase price. During holiday seasons (November-December), we offer premium gift wrapping options starting at $7.99.

PRODUCT REVIEWS
Customers can leave reviews within 60 days of purchase. Reviews must follow our community guidelines and be based on genuine product experience. Verified purchase reviews are highlighted with a badge. We do not remove negative reviews unless they violate our guidelines. Helpful reviews are rewarded with loyalty points.

INVENTORY AND AVAILABILITY
Product availability is shown in real-time on each product page. Items marked as "In Stock" typically ship within 24 hours. "Limited Stock" means fewer than 10 units remain. "Pre-Order" items have an estimated shipping date listed. We send restock notifications if you sign up for alerts on out-of-stock items.

PRIVACY AND DATA SECURITY
ShopSmart takes your privacy seriously. We collect only necessary information to process orders and improve your shopping experience. We never sell personal data to third parties. All payment information is encrypted and processed through PCI-compliant payment processors. You can request deletion of your account data at any time through account settings.
